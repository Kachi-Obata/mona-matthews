About Mona Matthews

ESTABLISHED 2002

Mona Matthews is a Nigerian footwear brand focused on timeless design, quality craftsmanship, and attention to detail. Each pair is thoughtfully made to balance elegance, comfort, and durability — designed for men and women who value style that lasts.

We are especially commited to creating beautifully proportioned footwear in larger sizes - ensuring that every man and woman with bigger feet experience the same confidence, grace, and refinement, without compromise.
